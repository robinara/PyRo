{\bfseries Z\+L\+A\+U\+U\+M} computes the product U\+U\+H or L\+H\+L, where U and L are upper or lower triangular matrices (blocked algorithm). 

 \begin{DoxyParagraph}{Purpose\+: }
\begin{DoxyVerb} ZLAUUM computes the product U * U**H or L**H * L, where the triangular
 factor U or L is stored in the upper or lower triangular part of
 the array A.

 If UPLO = 'U' or 'u' then the upper triangle of the result is stored,
 overwriting the factor U in A.
 If UPLO = 'L' or 'l' then the lower triangle of the result is stored,
 overwriting the factor L in A.

 This is the blocked form of the algorithm, calling Level 3 BLAS.\end{DoxyVerb}
 
\end{DoxyParagraph}
