\begin{DoxyVerb}A Gilbrat continuous random variable.

%(before_notes)s

Notes
-----
The probability density function for `gilbrat` is::

    gilbrat.pdf(x) = 1/(x*sqrt(2*pi)) * exp(-1/2*(log(x))**2)

`gilbrat` is a special case of `lognorm` with ``s = 1``.
