\subsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}A Rice continuous random variable.

%(before_notes)s

Notes
-----
The probability density function for `rice` is::

    rice.pdf(x, b) = x * exp(-(x**2+b**2)/2) * I[0](x*b)

for ``x > 0``, ``b > 0``.
