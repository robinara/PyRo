{\bfseries D\+L\+A\+R\+Z\+B} applies a block reflector or its transpose to a general matrix. 

 \begin{DoxyParagraph}{Purpose\+: }
\begin{DoxyVerb} DLARZB applies a real block reflector H or its transpose H**T to
 a real distributed M-by-N  C from the left or the right.

 Currently, only STOREV = 'R' and DIRECT = 'B' are supported.\end{DoxyVerb}
 
\end{DoxyParagraph}
