\subsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}A Nakagami continuous random variable.

%(before_notes)s

Notes
-----
The probability density function for `nakagami` is::

    nakagami.pdf(x, nu) = 2 * nu**nu / gamma(nu) *
                          x**(2*nu-1) * exp(-nu*x**2)

for ``x > 0``, ``nu > 0``.
