Notes
-----
The probability density function for `f` is::

                         df2**(df2/2) * df1**(df1/2) * x**(df1/2-1)
    F.pdf(x, df1, df2) = --------------------------------------------
                         (df2+df1*x)**((df1+df2)/2) * B(df1/2, df2/2)

for ``x > 0``.
