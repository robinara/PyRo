{\bfseries C\+L\+A\+Q\+R1} sets a scalar multiple of the first column of the product of 2-\/by-\/2 or 3-\/by-\/3 matrix H and specified shifts. 

 \begin{DoxyParagraph}{Purpose\+: }
\begin{DoxyVerb}      Given a 2-by-2 or 3-by-3 matrix H, CLAQR1 sets v to a
      scalar multiple of the first column of the product

      (*)  K = (H - s1*I)*(H - s2*I)

      scaling to avoid overflows and most underflows.

      This is useful for starting double implicit shift bulges
      in the QR algorithm.\end{DoxyVerb}
 
\end{DoxyParagraph}
