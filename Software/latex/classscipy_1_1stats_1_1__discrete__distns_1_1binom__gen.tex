\subsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}A binomial discrete random variable.

%(before_notes)s

Notes
-----
The probability mass function for `binom` is::

   binom.pmf(k) = choose(n, k) * p**k * (1-p)**(n-k)

for ``k`` in ``{0, 1,..., n}``.

`binom` takes ``n`` and ``p`` as shape parameters.
