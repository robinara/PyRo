\subsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}A generalized gamma continuous random variable.

%(before_notes)s

Notes
-----
The probability density function for `gengamma` is::

    gengamma.pdf(x, a, c) = abs(c) * x**(c*a-1) * exp(-x**c) / gamma(a)

for ``x > 0``, ``a > 0``, and ``c != 0``.
