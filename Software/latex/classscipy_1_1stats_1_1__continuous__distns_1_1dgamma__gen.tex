\subsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}A double gamma continuous random variable.

%(before_notes)s

Notes
-----
The probability density function for `dgamma` is::

    dgamma.pdf(x, a) = 1 / (2*gamma(a)) * abs(x)**(a-1) * exp(-abs(x))

for ``a > 0``.
